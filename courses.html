Introduction

Problem-solving is more than just a skill - it's a mindset, a way of approaching the world with curiosity, creativity, and determination. Whether you're an entrepreneur, a student, a parent, or a professional, you face challenges and obstacles every day that require problem-solving abilities. And the good news is that problem-solving can be learned and honed, just like any other skill.
